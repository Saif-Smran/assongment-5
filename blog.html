Question-1: What are the different ways to select an element in the DOM?

Answer: There are multiple ways to select an element in the DOM. Some of the most common ways are:

  • getElementById()
  • getElementsByClassName()
  • getElementsByTagName()
  • querySelector()
  • querySelectorAll()
